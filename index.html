What is the Ministry of Testing Columbus?

Quite simply we are a testing meetup.

The Ministry of Testing Columbus is a community of professionals, students, and enthusiasts that meet monthly to share their software testing experiences and vision for the future of testing. Our goal is to provide a welcoming environment for members of the community to speak and share their knowledge to all levels of software testers. 

Who is the Ministry of Testing?

The Ministry of Testing is a professional learning network. They've created a safe, inclusive, diverse and exciting community about all things testing.

Founded in 2007 in the UK, the Ministry of Testing is a community of software testers from all over the world, at different stages of their careers, with different skill sets and life goals. From humble beginnings as an online forum to the first TestBash conference in 2012, the Ministry of Testing has always focused on providing high value training and ideas to it's growing community of software professionals. For more information and access to free talks and training check out the Ministry of Testing's website.
